Water Lab

An original static-site ocean sandbox built for testing the interaction style of premium water demos.

Original implementation Static deploy Three.js

Included in this MVP

  • Animated ocean surface with crest foam
  • Preset-driven sky, lighting, and water mood
  • Floating buoy with bob/pitch/roll sampling
  • Seabed glow + faux caustics
  • Underwater mode and orbit camera
Note

This is not a copy of Three.js Water Pro. It is a fresh public prototype intended for Paperclip testing and iteration.
